Fair splitting rule
===================

[[Parent]]: splitrules.txt

The fair rule chooses to split along the longest side of the node.
On this axis it chooses to split at the midpoint of the 
minimum bounding interval of the contained points.

References
----------

_A decomposition of Multidimensional Point Sets with
Applications to k-Nearest-Neighbors and n-Body
Potential Fields_,
Paul B. Callahan, S. Rao. Kosaraju,
Journal of the ACM, Volume 42, Number 1, pages 67-90, 
January 1995.
