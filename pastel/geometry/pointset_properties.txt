Point-set properties
====================

[[Parent]]: pastelgeometry.txt

Theory
------

A _point-set S in ''RR^n''_ is simply a subset ''S sub RR^n'',
with no restrictions on cardinality.

References
----------

Most of the algorithms under this section 
(excluding eigenstructure of a point-set), are explained 
for 2- and 3-dimensional cases in:

_Geometric Tools for Computer Graphics_, Philip J. Schneider, David Eberly, 2003.
