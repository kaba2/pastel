Sphere
======

[[Parent]]: shapes.txt
[[set indent]]: EquationSet

A subset ''S sub RR^n'' is called a _sphere_, if there exists
a point ''c in RR^n'' and ''r in RR, r >= 0'' such that 

	S = {x in RR^n: |x - c| <= r},

where the norm is Euclidean. This is a closed sphere; an open
sphere is given by the condition ''< r''.
