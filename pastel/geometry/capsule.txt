Capsule
=======

[Parent]: shapes.txt

Theory
------

A _capsule_ ''S'' in ''RR^n'' is a Minkowski sum of a closed line segment 
''L'' and a closed ''r''-radius ball. Alternatively,
if ''A'' and ''B'' are the end-points of ''L'', then 

''S = {x in RR^n | exists t in [0, 1] sub RR: |(A + t(B - A)) - x| <= r}''

where the norm is Euclidean.
