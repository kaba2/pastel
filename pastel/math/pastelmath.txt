PastelMath
==========

[[Parent]]: pastel.txt

_PastelMath_ provides general-purpose mathematical tools. Algorithms 
and data structures for geometry can be found from the 
[PastelGeometry][Geometry] sublibrary. Some of the basic mathematical
tools can be found from [PastelSys][Sys] sublibrary.
