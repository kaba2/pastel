Grid traversal
==============

[[Parent]]: graph_algorithms.txt

A _grid traversal_ traverses all the points in an aligned box ''B subset ZZ^n''. A _row-major_ grid-traversal traverses the points in a lexicographical order. A _column-major_ grid-traversal traverses the point in a reverse lexicographical order, that is, a lexicographical order in ''(x_n, ..., x_1)''.
