Gaussian-distributed random numbers
===================================

[[Parent]]: random_continuous.txt

The probability density function of a gaussian-distributed
random variable is given by:

''f(x) = 1 / (sqrt(2 pi sigma^2))    e^(-(x - mu)^2 / (2 sigma^2))''
